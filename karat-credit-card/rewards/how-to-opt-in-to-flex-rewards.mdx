---
title: "How to opt in to Flex Rewards"
---

To activate Flex Rewards and start earning 3x on all your spending, you'll need to set up autopay with a linked bank account.

## Requirements

Before opting in to Flex Rewards, you must:
- Have an active Karat Card
- Link a bank account for autopay
- Meet the [eligibility requirements](/karat-credit-card/rewards/flex-rewards-eligibility)

## How to opt in

### From your dashboard

1. Log in to your Karat dashboard
2. Look for the "Enable 3X Rewards" button on your home page
3. Click the button to open the Flex Rewards enrollment flow
4. Review the program details in the carousel
5. Select your autopay bank account (or add a new one)
6. Review and accept the program terms
7. Click "Accept & Activate 3X Rewards"

Once activated, you'll immediately start earning 3x rewards on all eligible purchases, and daily repayments will begin automatically.

### Selecting your autopay account

During enrollment, you'll choose which bank account to use for daily automatic repayments. You can:
- Select an existing linked bank account
- Add a new bank account through Plaid

Make sure your selected account has sufficient funds to cover your daily Karat Card balance.

## What happens after you opt in

Once you've activated Flex Rewards:
- You'll earn 3x rewards on all eligible Karat Card purchases
- Your card balance will be automatically paid down daily from your autopay account
- You'll receive a confirmation that 3X Card Rewards are enabled
- You can view your daily repayment schedule in your dashboard

## Changing your autopay account

You can change your autopay account at any time in your account settings. Daily repayments will continue using your newly selected account.

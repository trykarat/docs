## How Flex Rewards differs from the standard rewards program

**Flex Rewards:**
- Earn 3x rewards on **every eligible purchase**, regardless of merchant category
- Requires autopay to be enabled with daily automatic repayments
- No need to select or manage reward categories

**Standard rewards program:**
- Earn 1-3x rewards on **three categories** of your choice
- Standard monthly billing cycle
- You select and can change your reward categories

## Key benefits

With Flex Rewards, you get:
- **Simplified rewards**: No need to track categories or optimize spending
- **Maximum earning potential**: 3x on all eligible purchases means you never miss out on rewards
- **Automatic repayments**: Your balance is automatically paid down daily from your linked bank account
- **Better cash flow**: Daily repayments help you manage your available credit more effectively

---
title: "What is Plaid?"
---

Plaid is a product that lets users securely connect bank accounts to apps. Plaid is used by companies like American Express and Venmo to authenticate and verify bank accounts instantly. You can learn more about it at [<u>Plaid.com</u>](http://Plaid.com).

Karat uses Plaid to set the right limits on your card and to set up direct deposit repayment. Plaid processes your account savings, income and expenses, account and routing number, etc.

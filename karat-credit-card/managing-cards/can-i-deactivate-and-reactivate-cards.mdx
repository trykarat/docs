---
title: "Can I deactivate and reactivate cards?"
---

Yes—as long as your account is in good standing and you don’t have a balance owed.

[<u>In the app</u>](https://app.trykarat.com/signin), navigate to **Credit Card \>** **Cards** page via the menu on the left, then click the checkbox for the card you want to deactivate or reactivate under the **Status** column.

title: "How do I contact Karat Banking customer support? What hours are they available?"
---

You can reach the Karat Concierge support team Monday - Friday from 7 AM to 5 PM PT, and on weekends from 8 AM to 5 PM PT. We're available via email at [<u>concierge@trykarat.com</u>](mailto:concierge@trykarat.com), or you can call or text us at (323) 747-7086.\
​

Need quick help? You can also log into your account and chat with us live! Our chat support is available daily from 9 AM to 5 PM PT.\
​

Please note: We may be offline during certain bank holidays. If so, you’ll receive an automated message with details on when we’ll be back to assist you!

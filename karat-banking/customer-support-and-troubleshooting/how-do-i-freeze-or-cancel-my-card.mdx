---
title: "How do I freeze or cancel my card?"
---

1. Log in to your account at [<u>app.trykarat.com/signin</u>](http://app.trykarat.com/signin) using your credentials.
2. Once logged in, click on the **Banking** tab.
3. Scroll down to the **Cards** section located on the right-hand side.
4. Find the virtual or physical card that you want to manage.
5. Click the three vertical dots (⋮) next to the card. You will see two options: Freeze or Cancel.
   - **Freeze**:
     - Your card will be temporarily frozen and cannot be used until you unfreeze it.
     - Recommended if you’ve just misplaced your card and think you may find it.
     - You can unfreeze anytime in the dashboard.
   - **Cancel:**
     - Your card will be permanently deactivated and this action cannot be undone.
     - Recommended if your card is lost, stolen, or if you’ve noticed fraudulent charges.
6. To **Freeze** your card, click Freeze and click Confirm freeze card **to finalize.**
7. To **Cancel** your card, Click Cancel and then Confirm cancel card to finalize.
   - _If you cancel your card, please reach out to Karat Concierge via email at _[_<u>concierge@trykarat.com</u>_](mailto:concierge@trykarat.com)_ or via text/call at _[_<u>(323) 747-7086</u>_](tel:3237477086)_ to request a new one._

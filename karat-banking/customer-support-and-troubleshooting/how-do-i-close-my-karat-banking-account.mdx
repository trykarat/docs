title: "How do I close my Karat Banking account?"
---

You can email our Concierge team at [<u>concerige@trykarat.com</u>](mailto:concerige@trykarat.com) to request to close your account. Karat reserves the right to not close accounts that have negative balances. See all terms and conditions in our [<u>Deposit Account Agreement</u>](http://trykarat.com/deposit-account-agreement).

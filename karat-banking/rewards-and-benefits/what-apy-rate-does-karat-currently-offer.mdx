---
title: "What APY rate does Karat currently offer?"
---

As of December 2025 Karat offers up to 1.75% APY on checking deposits for Karat Premium members and up to 2.75% APY on deposits in our Automatic Tax Planning product for Karat Premium members. Karat Basic Banking members can earn up to 0.75% APY on their Automatic Tax Planning account.

Please note that rates are tied to rate set by the Federal Reserve and are subject to change.

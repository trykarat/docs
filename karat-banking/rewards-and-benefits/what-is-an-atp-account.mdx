---

An Automated Tax Planning (ATP) account is an additional account we offer alongside your checking account designed to help you prepare for tax season by automatically setting aside a portion of your income.

You can choose to have up to 50% of your income based deposits, such as YouTube Adsense payments or brand deals, automatically routed into your ATP account. Funds are pulled directly from deposits made to your checking account.

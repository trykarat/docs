---
title: "What happens if I cancel during a free trial?"
---

Need to cancel your free trial? Here’s what happens:

- Your KP subscription will end immediately.
- Yield stops accruing.
- All earned yield will be paid on the 1st of the following month.

If you decide to resubscribe to Karat Premium, you can do so anytime under ‘Manage Karat Premium Subscription’.

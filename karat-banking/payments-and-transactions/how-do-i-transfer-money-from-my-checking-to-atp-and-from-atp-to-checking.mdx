title: "How do I transfer money from my Checking to ATP? And from ATP to Checking?"
---

To transfer funds, follow these steps:

1. Log in to your dashboard at [<u>app.trykarat.com/banking</u>](http://app.trykarat.com/banking)
2. Go to Move Money \> Send Money \> Internal Transfer.
3. Choose the direction of the transfer (e.g., From Checking to Tax or From Tax to Checking).
4. Click Confirm, enter the amount you want to transfer, then click Continue.

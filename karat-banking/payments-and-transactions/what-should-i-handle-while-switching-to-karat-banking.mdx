---
title: "What should I handle while switching to Karat Banking?"
---

Switching banks can feel like a big step, but we’ve got you covered with this simple to do list to make your transition smooth!

1. Open Your New Bank Account with Karat
   - Apply at [<u>trykarat.com</u>](http://trykarat.com)
   - Once approved, you'll receive an update via text and email.
   - Fund your Karat banking account in your dashboard at [<u>app.trykarat.com/signin</u>](http://app.trykarat.com/signin) by clicking 'Add Money'.
   - You can start using your virtual card right away while you wait for your new card!​
2. Switch over any Auto-Payments and Deposits. For a more comprehensive instruction on how to do this, check out: [**<u>Switching Your Subscriptions to Karat</u>**](/karat-banking/payments-and-transactions/switching-your-subscriptions-to-karat)
   - Direct deposits (e.g. payroll, brand payouts, etc).


   - Automatic bill payments (business services, utilities, subscriptions, etc).
   - Linked services (PayPal, Venmo, Cash App, etc.).
3. Move Your Money
   - Transfer over any balance you don’t want to keep in your old account.
   - Keep enough in your old account to cover pending transactions.
4. Switch Direct Deposits
   - Update any client or brands that use direct deposit with your new bank info.
5. Update Bill Payments & Subscriptions
   - Utilities (gas, electric, water)
   - Rent or mortgage
   - Insurance
   - Streaming services
   - Credit cards
   - Any other recurring payments

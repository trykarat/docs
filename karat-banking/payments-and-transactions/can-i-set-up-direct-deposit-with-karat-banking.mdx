---
title: "Can I set up direct deposit with Karat Banking?"
---

To set up direct deposit, you can find your account ACH details on the homepage of the app or via the “Send money” feature. You’re able to set up direct deposit with any service that supports it, like YouTube, Instagram, Twitch, Shopify, and more.

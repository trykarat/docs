title: "What specific Banking access and permission does each role have?"
---

Check out the table below for a detailed breakdown of the permissions assigned to each role for Banking users:

| **Permission**            | **Admin**                          | **Manager**                        | **Employee**                           | **Accountant**                |
| :------------------------ | :--------------------------------- | :--------------------------------- | :------------------------------------- | :---------------------------- |
| View account balances     | ✅ All                              | ✅ All                              | ✅                                      | ✅ All                         |
| View transactions         | ✅ All                              | ✅ All                              | ✅ Own only                             | ✅ All                         |
| Initiate money movement   | ✅                                  | ✅ (no limit)                       | ✅ (Within set limit, or with approval) | ❌ Can view & copy ACH details |
| View business information | ✅                                  | ✅                                  | ❌                                      | ❌                             |
| Edit business information | ✅                                  | ❌                                  | ❌                                      | ❌                             |
| Bookkeeping & reporting   | ✅                                  | ✅                                  | ❌                                      | ✅                             |
| Manage team members       | ✅                                  | ❌                                  | ❌                                      | ❌                             |
| Banking cards             | ✅                                  | ✅                                  | Only their own                         | ❌                             |
| Access ATP tools          | ✅                                  | ✅                                  | ❌                                      | ✅                             |
| Access perks              | ✅                                  | ✅                                  | ✅                                      | ✅                             |
| Karat Premium             | Full access (opt-in, cancel, etc.) | Full access (opt-in, cancel, etc.) | ❌                                      | ❌                             |

For Card roles and permissions, you can navigate here: [<u>What specific Card access and permission does each role have?</u>](/karat-banking/user-roles-and-permissions/what-specific-card-access-and-permission-does-each-role-have)

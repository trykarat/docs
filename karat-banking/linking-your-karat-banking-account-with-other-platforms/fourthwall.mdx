---
title: "Fourthwall"
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log into Fourthwall">
    - Go to [**<u>fourthwall.com</u>**](http://fourthwall.com) and sign in to your creator account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Settings">
    - Click your profile icon in the bottom-left corner (or top-right if on mobile), then select Settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Payments">
    - In the left-hand menu, click Payments or Payouts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage Payout Method">
    - You’ll be redirected to Stripe, which handles Fourthwall’s payouts.
    - Click "Manage your payout settings" or similar to open your Stripe dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update Bank Info in Stripe">
    - In Stripe, go to Payout Settings → Bank accounts and scheduling.
    - Click Edit next to your existing bank account, or Add bank account if switching.
    - Enter your new bank account details (routing number, account number, etc.).
    - Save the changes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify the Change (if required)
">
    - Stripe may ask you to verify the new bank account with microdeposits or ID re-verification.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Payout Schedule">
    - Double-check that your payout schedule is still active and correct.
  </Step>
</Steps>

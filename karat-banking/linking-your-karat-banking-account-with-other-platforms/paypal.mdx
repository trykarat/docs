title: "PayPal"
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log In to Your PayPal Account">
    - Go to [<u>https://www.paypal.com</u>](https://www.paypal.com) and log in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Wallet">
    - **Desktop:** Click "Wallet" at the top of the screen.
    - **Mobile:** Tap the Menu icon, then tap "Wallet."
  </Step>
  <Step title="Remove the Old Bank Account (Optional)">
    - Under "Bank accounts," click your old account.
    - Select "Remove bank account" if you no longer plan to use it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Link Your New Bank Account">
    - In the Wallet section, click or tap "Link a bank account."
    - Use Plaid to link your account, orâ€¦
    - Choose the account type: Checking or Savings.
    - Enter your new bank's routing number and account number.
    - Confirm and save.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Your Bank Account">
    - PayPal will make two small deposits (usually \< \$1) to your new account within 1-3 business days.
    - Once you see these deposits, return to Wallet.
    - Click your new bank account, then "Confirm," and enter the deposit amounts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Your New Bank as Primary (if needed)">
    - After confirmation, select your new bank account as the primary withdrawal method if PayPal didn't do so automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Your future AdSense payouts will now go to your new account. Payouts are typically issued between the 21st and 26th of each month, provided you meet the payment threshold.

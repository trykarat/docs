title: "Patreon"
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log In to Your Patreon Account">
    - Go to [<u>https://www.patreon.com</u>](https://www.patreon.com) and log in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access Payout Settings">
    - Click your **profile photo** (top-right).
    - Go to **"Settings."**
    - In the left sidebar, click **"Payouts and taxes."**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Payout Method">
    - Youâ€™ll see your **current payout method** (e.g., PayPal, direct deposit).
    - Click the **"Edit"** button next to it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Remove or Replace the Old Method">
    - You may need to **remove** the old payout method first.
    - Click **"Remove"** or choose **"Add a new payout method."**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Your New Bank Account">
    - Choose **Direct Deposit** (for U.S. only).
    - Enter your **bank account number, routing number**, and **other required info**.
    - For U.S. accounts, you'll need:
      - **Account Type** (Checking/Savings)
      - **Routing Number**
      - **Account Number**
      - **Bank Name**
      - **Address info**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Your Identity (if prompted)">
    - Patreon may require **identity verification** (via Persona) to comply with payment regulations.
    - Follow the on-screen steps.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and Confirm">
    - After entering the new info, hit **Save**.
    - Your payouts will now be sent to the new bank account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Note: Payouts typically occur **on the 5th of each month** or **manually on demand**, depending on your settings.
</Note>

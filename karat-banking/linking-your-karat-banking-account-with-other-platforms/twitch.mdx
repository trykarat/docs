title: "Twitch"
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your Twitch Dashboard">
    - Go to [<u>https://dashboard.twitch.tv/</u>](https://dashboard.twitch.tv/) and log in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Affiliate or Partner Settings
">
    - [<u>https://dashboard.twitch.tv/settings/revenue</u>](https://dashboard.twitch.tv/settings/revenue)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click on “Change Payout Method”">
    - Scroll to the “Onboarding” section
    - Click “Change Payout Method”
    - Open the Payment Methods dropdown and select Direct Deposit / ACH.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Your New Bank Account">
    - Enter your new bank account details:
      1. Account holder name
      2. Bank name
      3. Routing number
      4. Account number
    - Double-check for typos.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and confirm">
    - Make sure your changes are saved
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Note: Your future Twitch payouts will now go to your new account. Payouts are typically issued on or around the 15th of the month, provided you meet the payout threshold.
</Note>

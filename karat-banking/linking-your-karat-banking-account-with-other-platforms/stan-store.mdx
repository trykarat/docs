---
title: "Stan Store"
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log into Stan Store">
    - Go to [<u>stan.store</u>](http://stan.store) and sign in to your creator account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the Dashboard">
    - Once logged in, click your profile icon in the top-right and select Dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Earnings / Payout Settings">
    - In the left sidebar, look for Earnings, Payouts, or Settings. Click it.
    - Then choose Payout Settings or Bank Details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage Payouts via Stripe">
    - Stan Store uses Stripe to process payouts.
    - Click the link or button that says “Manage with Stripe” or “Update bank account”.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update Your Info in Stripe">
    - Stripe will open in a new tab.
    - In your Stripe Dashboard, go to: Settings → Payouts and Bank Accounts
    - Click Edit next to your current bank account, or Add new.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your New Bank Details
">
    - Fill in your new account number, routing number, and save the changes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Your Account">
    - Stripe might send microdeposits to confirm your new bank account. Follow their steps if prompted.
  </Step>
</Steps>

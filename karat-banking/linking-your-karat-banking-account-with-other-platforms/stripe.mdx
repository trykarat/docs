<Accordion title="For Stripe Express" description="Instructions for updating your Stripe connection for platforms that use a Stripe Express setup including TikTok, Gumroad, Substack, Patreon, Teachable, Podia, Ko-fi, Fourthwall, and more.">
  Used by TikTok, Gumroad, Substack, Patreon, Teachable, Podia, Ko-fi, Fourthwall, and more

  <Steps>
    <Step title="Find the Platform That Uses Stripe">
      - Open the relevant creator platform (e.g., Ko-fi, Stan Store).
      - Go to your **Payouts** or **Payment Settings** section.
      - Click **Manage with Stripe** or **Access Stripe Dashboard** — this opens your **Stripe Express** account.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Open Payout Settings">
      - In Stripe Express, click the **menu icon** (≡) or go to **Settings**.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Update Bank Info">
      - Follow [<u>this guide from Stripe</u>](https://support.stripe.com/questions/update-existing-bank-account-information) to find where to change your info.
      - Click **Edit**, or **\+ Add Bank Account**.
      - Enter your **new bank account info** (routing + account numbers).
      - Save and verify if needed.
    </Step>
  </Steps>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="For Stripe Standard Dashboard" description="Instructions for setting up using the Stripe Standard Dashboard experience used by fewer and less common platforms.">
  Less common, but still used

  <Steps>
    <Step title="Go to dashboard.stripe.com">
      - Log in with your Stripe email (this only works if you’ve set up a full Standard Stripe account) from [dashboard.stripe.com](http://dashboard.stripe.com/).
    </Step>
    <Step title="Navigate to Settings">
      - Bottom left → Click **Settings** → Under "Payments and Payouts," choose **Bank accounts and scheduling**.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Update Bank Info">
      - Edit your existing bank account or add a new one.
      - Stripe may ask you to verify ownership.
    </Step>
  </Steps>
</Accordion>

---
title: "X/Twitter"
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to X">
    - Go to [<u>x.com</u>](http://x.com) and log in to the account that receives payouts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access Monetization Settings">
    - Click on your profile picture (bottom left on desktop).
    - Select “Monetization”.
    - Choose the program you’re enrolled in (e.g., Ads Revenue Sharing, Subscriptions).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Stripe Dashboard">
    - X uses Stripe for payouts.
    - Under your monetization settings, click the link or button that says “Payout Settings” or “View in Stripe”. This will redirect you to your Stripe Express Dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update Bank Account in Stripe">
    - Once in Stripe, go to **Settings** (gear icon in the top-right).
    - Under “Payout and professional details,” click “Bank accounts and scheduling.”
    - Click “Edit” next to your current bank information.
    - Enter your new bank account details and save.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Note: Stripe may send a confirmation or require re-verification. Ensure your account is verified to avoid payout delays.
</Note>

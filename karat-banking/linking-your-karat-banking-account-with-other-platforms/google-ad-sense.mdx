---
title: "Google AdSense"
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in to Google AdSense">
    - Go to [<u>https://www.google.com/adsense</u>](https://www.google.com/adsense)
    - Use the Google account associated with your AdSense earnings.
    - Access Payments Settings
    - In the left-hand sidebar, click “Payments.”
    - Then click “Payment info.”
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Manage Payment Methods">
    - Scroll down to the “How you get paid” section.
    - Click “Manage payment methods.”
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Your New Bank Account">
    - Click “Add payment method.”
    - Enter your new bank account details:
      - Account holder name
      - Bank name
      - Routing number
      - Account number
    - **Double-check for typos.**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the New Account as Primary">
    - Once added, click “Set as primary” next to your new bank account.
    - _Optional:_ You can delete the old account if you're no longer using it.

      Verify if Required
    - Depending on your country, AdSense may deposit a small test amount (e.g., \$0.01–\$1.00) into the new account.
    - If prompted, return to AdSense and enter the exact amount to verify the account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Note: Your future AdSense payouts will now go to your new account. Payouts are typically issued between the 21st and 26th of each month, provided you meet the payment threshold.
</Note>

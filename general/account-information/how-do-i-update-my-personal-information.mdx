---
title: "How do I update my personal information?"
---

In the app, go to the **Settings** page, then scroll to the section you want to update.

At the top of the page you’ll see **Personal Information**, which includes your name and phone number. When you’ve finished updating your info, click **Update Personal Info** to save it.

At the bottom of the page, you can update your billing and mailing addresses by filling out the form with your new details, then clicking the **Update Billing Address** or **Update Mailing Address** button.
